# Templates

In-panel walkthrough. See [Guide / Templates](/guide/templates) for the
concepts.

## Browsing

Templates are categorised. The panel ships with a starter set; your saved
templates appear in **Personnels**.

## Saving from any entity / interaction

Open the entity editor, click **⋯ → Sauvegarder comme template**. Pick a
name and category.

## Importing / exporting

Each template is a JSON blob. Export with **Exporter** (copies to
clipboard), import with **+ Importer** (paste).

This is the recommended way to ship content between your own servers
without dumping SQL.
